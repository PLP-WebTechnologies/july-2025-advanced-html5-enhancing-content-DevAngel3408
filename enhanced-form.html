Top 5 Healthy Kenyan Foods

  1. Sukuma Wiki – Rich in vitamins A and C
  2. Ugali – Energy-giving staple made from maize flour
  3. Omena – Excellent source of calcium and protein
  4. Ndengu – Great for plant-based protein
  5. Sweet Potatoes – High in fiber and antioxidants

Daily Healthy Living Checklist

  • Drink at least 2 liters of water daily
  • Walk or cycle for 30 minutes
  • Eat at least 2 servings of vegetables
  • Get enough rest (7–9 hours)
